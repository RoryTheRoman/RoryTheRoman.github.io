Jenny Lewis Fan Site

Child film star turned indie rockband frontrunner, Jenny Lewis is now a solo/collaborative recording artist.

Blending elements of rock, folk, and country, Lewis' music rocks as much as it croons, often with incredibly personal themes that resonate with many. She has come a long way since cofounding Rilo Kiley in 1998. And, though she has been a part of a few joint projects, her music really shines when she is in charge.
